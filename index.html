THE BEATMAKING MANUAL

A free, no-fluff reference for FL Studio producers — built from real sessions, not textbooks. Drums, 808s, mixing, EQ, arrangement, music theory, sound design, and mastering, organized so you can jump straight to what you need.

I made this because I wanted something I could hand to a new producer and say "start here." No courses to buy, no gatekeeping, no upsells — just the stuff that actually matters, free for anyone who wants it. If it helps you, pass it on.
